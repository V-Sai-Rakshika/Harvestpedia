About Harvestpedia

Where Nature Meets Knowledge

We believe that understanding what you eat is the first step toward living better. Harvestpedia exists to make plant-based nutrition knowledge accessible, accurate, and inspiring — for everyone.

Our Mission

Built for a healthier world

Harvestpedia was created to bridge the gap between nature's abundance and everyday health choices.

Why Harvestpedia exists

Nutrition information today is scattered, overly technical, or buried behind paywalls. We set out to build a single, trustworthy platform where anyone — a student, a parent, a health enthusiast — can look up any fruit, vegetable, or plant and instantly understand its value.

Our focus is plant-based knowledge: what foods grow in nature, what they contain, and how they benefit the human body. We cover everything from common grocery staples to rare seasonal varieties — all presented in plain, accessible language.

Harvestpedia is free, open, and constantly growing. We are committed to keeping knowledge available to all, without barriers.

Health First

Evidence-based information

Every nutritional fact on Harvestpedia is grounded in established dietary science. We avoid trends and focus on what the evidence actually supports.

Accessibility

Simple language, deep insight

We translate complex nutritional data into language anyone can understand — without oversimplifying the science behind it.

Plant-based

Celebrating nature's diversity

From tropical fruits to humble root vegetables, we document the full spectrum of plant-based foods and their unique contributions to human health.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy, clearly explained

We keep this simple. No legal jargon — just a straightforward explanation of how we handle your information.

What we collect Minimal

Harvestpedia does not require you to create an account to use the platform. We do not collect personal information unless you choose to contact us directly through our contact form.

When you use our site, standard server logs may record your browser type and approximate region purely for performance and security monitoring. This data is never sold or shared.

How we use data Transparently

Any information you provide — such as your name and email via the contact form — is used solely to respond to your message. We do not subscribe you to mailing lists without your explicit consent.

We may use anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (such as which pages are visited most) to improve the platform. This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Cookies & Tracking Non-intrusive

Harvestpedia uses only essential cookies necessary for basic site functionality, such as remembering your seasonal filter preference. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking tools that profile your behavior across the web.

We do not display advertisements and we do not sell user data. Your visit to Harvestpedia is your own.

Third-party services Limited

Our platform may load fonts and images from trusted external sources such as Google Fonts and Unsplash. These services operate under their own privacy policies. We choose partners carefully and limit external dependencies wherever possible.

Your rights Always respected

You have the right to request deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us. Simply email us at privacy@harvestpedia.com and we will action your request promptly. If you have any questions about this policy, we are happy to clarify.
